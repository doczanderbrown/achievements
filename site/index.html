About FCSPA

Mission, Governance, and Professional Standards

FCSPA is structured to operate with clear governance, transparent policies, and education priorities that raise the standard of sterile processing in our region.

Core Objectives

Increase regional certification Provide accessible CE opportunities Strengthen AAMI and AORN literacy Support IFU and CDC compliance Build future SPD leadership Promote professional recognition Coordinate hospital, ASC, and vendor collaboration

Education Model

  • Quarterly meetings minimum, with monthly cadence preferred
  • Structured agendas with standards-focused presentations
  • Attendance tracking and post-event CE documentation
  • Topic selection tied to real SPD operational risk and compliance gaps

Priority CE Topics

  • IFU interpretation challenges and compliance workflows
  • AAMI tray weight standards and IUSS reduction strategies
  • Decontamination compliance metrics and sterilizer documentation trends
  • High-level disinfection updates and case-study review sessions
